Help & Support FAQ

How does the priority queue work?
Orders are prioritized based on payment status (Paid orders first) and pickup urgency. Early pickup times get processed first.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, but only for "Pay on Pickup" orders and while the status is still "Pending". Paid orders or orders already processing cannot be cancelled.
What file formats are supported?
We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, PNG, JPG, and JPEG. We recommend PDF to avoid formatting layout mismatches.
What if I miss my scheduled pickup time?
Your prints will be kept safely at the shop counter for up to 24 hours. Show your token at the desk to collect them.
